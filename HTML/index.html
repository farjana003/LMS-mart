Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zeppto a payment service provider?

Zeppto is not a payment service provider. Zeppto activates a backup payment provider (e.g. Stripe) in order to save your failed payments. Zeppto can also activate additional payment service providers. Contact us for any inquiry.

What will my customers see during a retry?

During an instant payment retry we notify your customers that their payment is being processed. No action is required on their side. If the payment is saved, your customer will be redirected to your standard confirmation page.

Which countries and currencies does Zeppto support?

Zeppto supports all countries and all currencies offered by our payment partners.

Does Zeppto affect website performance?

Zeppto's solution can be loaded asynchronously and therefore does not affect the performance of your website.
